Farmhouse FAQ

Everything you need to know about your countryside getaway

🏡

How many guests can stay at the farmhouse?

Our farmhouse can comfortably accommodate up to 12 guests with 4 bedrooms, each with authentic rustic charm and modern amenities.

🌾

What farm activities are included?

Enjoy morning farm walks, animal feeding, fresh egg collection, vegetable harvesting, and seasonal fruit picking right from our organic gardens.

🍽️

Are meals provided during the stay?

We offer farm-to-table dining with fresh, organic ingredients. Breakfast and dinner are included, featuring local specialties and homegrown produce.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

Is the farmhouse child-friendly?

Absolutely! We have safe play areas, friendly farm animals, nature trails, and plenty of space for kids to explore and learn about rural life.

🌻

What's the best time to visit?

Each season offers unique experiences - spring planting, summer harvests, autumn colors, and cozy winter evenings by the fireplace.

🚗

How do we reach the farmhouse?

Located just 90 minutes from the city center via well-maintained country roads. We provide detailed directions and can arrange pickup services.
